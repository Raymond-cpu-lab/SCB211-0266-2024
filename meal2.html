Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 2 onions
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Fresh coriander
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and sauté the chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger paste, and sauté for another minute.
  3. Add the curry powder and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh coriander. Serve with rice or naan bread.
